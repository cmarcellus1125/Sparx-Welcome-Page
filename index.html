Welcome to Sparx

Sparx is a positive, inclusive, and safe community of learners where students can research, create, collaborate, collect and present school projects and original works. It's social media for school!

Create and share your work

Use Sparx to make portfolios of your work: for assignments, for college applications, for jobs, or for fun! Present your work on the Live Feed or in forums, or be inspired by others’ work. Its you who’s in control!

Make a page of your interests

Whether its planes, plants, or poetry, with Sparx you’ll find tons of spaces to be yourself while you create and learn! Start your own page or join a group and let the geeking begin!

Start and join groups

Sparx can be a place for your finest creations, a place to find a friendly group to share ideas, or a quiet space to work on and save your favorite projects!
